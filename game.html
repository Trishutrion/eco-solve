Eco-Quest: Part 1

An environmental disaster has occured!
Water-soluble aliens have made their way from outer space into clouds in Earth's atmosphere,
so they fall to the Earth alongside rain from these clouds.
They then permeate into the soil and cause intense erosion, damaging crop yields and floral biodiversity.
In short, Earth is under attack!

However, your team of scientists has discovered a solution. If you trap the aliens in nitric acid, they will be paralysed, hence reducing the threat they pose to the environment.
So, on a rainy day, your team creates a giant vat of nitric acid and sets off, hoping to catch some aliens in the process.
