About Eco-Solve

Eco-Solve is an environmental education website where users can learn more about the environment and make more sustainable choices.
To do this, users can watch videos, take quizzes, play games, and even set their own personal goals.

Videos

 The videos page of Eco-Solve is for all visual learners wanting to learn more about the environment.
Currently, the only video available is the award-winning short film "Echoes of Humanity",
a dystopian tale about the last human alive leaving a message about how humanity destroyed the environment.

Quizzes

 Want to test yourself?
Eco-Solve's quiz webpage is sure to provide both a challenge and a benchmark for how well you are progressing.
The quizzes are 5-10 questions long and cover topics about environmental science.
The questions are in a random order, so be prepared for whatever comes your way.

Games

 For some downtime and fun, Eco-Solve offers a mini-game called Eco-Quest.
In the game, there is a tank that collects greenhouse gas particles, and the more particles that are collected, the more the temperature drops, hence the particles will fall faster.
The time left and the temperature will be displayed on the screen. The arrow keys or the 'a' and 'd' keys can move the tank, but the tank cannot leave the game screen.
The game aims to collect as many greenhouse gas particles as possible within one minute. How many particles can you collect? Let's find out!

Personal Tracker

 Using the personal tracker feature of Eco-Solve, it is now possible to check your current environmental footprint by entering data into our tracker.
In addition, you can set your own sustainability goals and reduce your negative impact on the environment.
We value privacy at Eco-Solve, so your data is stored in a .csv file and is not accessible to anyone else (even us, for that matter).
